My Vision & Ambition

I am an exceptionally self-driven and strategic learner with a clear vision for my future. My goal is to become a world-class engineer and a pioneer in technology, following in the footsteps of great innovators. I am on a mission to become one of the most advanced students in Kenya for my age, and I am dedicated to using my skills to solve real-world problems in Africa.

I am not just learning to code; I am preparing to become a future ambassador for STEM and innovation in Kenya, much like Wangari Maathai was for the environment. I believe that technology has the power to transform my country and my continent, and I want to be at the forefront of that change.

Future Plans

My academic and career goals are ambitious and well-defined. I plan to start my studies at the University of the People in September 2025 and complete the entire MIT OpenCourseWare EECS track before I turn 16. With a professional-grade portfolio, I will be applying to top universities like MIT, Stanford, and Carnegie Mellon, as well as prestigious internships and global competitions such as the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) and the International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF).
